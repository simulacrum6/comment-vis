Upload Format Explanation

Example:

{{example | json}}

Explanations:

comment: raw comment that was extracted
aspect: topic that was talked about in the comment, consisting of the raw text detail from which this topic was extracted and the group it was assigned to
attribute: trait indicating the evaluation of this topic in this comment, again consisting of the text and the group
sentiment: evaluation of this opinion. Can be: positive, neutral, negative, unknown. Default: unknown
